About iHub Africa

We create practical pathways into digital work through technicaltraining, meta skills, mentorship, and community.

Learn More

Mission

To equip young people with practical digital skills, character, and confidence for meaningful work.

Vision

An Africa where young people are creators, contributors,and leaders in the digital future.

The standards behind the work

Four principles guide how iHub Africa trains, supports, and partners.

Empowerment

Agency, skills, and confidence that help young people grow.

Integrity

Responsible work and honest growth.

Innovation

Modern problem solving for real needs.

Inclusion

Opportunity that welcomes young people in.

From applications to opportunity
